Radio

Symantically define radio buttons in both vertical and horizontal orientations.

Horizontal

Choose your interests 
                        
<fieldset>
    <legend>Choose your interests</legend>
    <input type="radio" name="interests" id="coding" value="coding" checked />
    <label for="coding">Coding</label>
    <input type="radio" name="interests" id="music" value="music" />
    <label for="music">Music</label>
</fieldset>

Vertical

Choose your interests 
                        
<fieldset>
    <legend>Choose your interests</legend>
    <label>
        <input type="checkbox" name="interests" value="coding" checked />
        Coding
    </label>
    <label>
        <input type="checkbox" name="interests" value="music" />
        Music
    </label>
</fieldset>
